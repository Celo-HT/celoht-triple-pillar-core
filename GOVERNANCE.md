# Governance Model — CeloHT

## Governance Principles
- Transparency  
- Community Oversight  
- Accountability  
- Ethical Standards  

## Organizational Structure
- Governor Council  
- Education Lead  
- Agent Network Lead  
- Reforestation Lead  
- Treasury Manager  
- Compliance Officer  

## Voting System
- Simple majority for standard proposals
- ⅔ supermajority for financial decisions
- Public proposal system: CEP (CeloHT Enhancement Proposals)

## Treasury
- cUSD-based
- Multi-signature wallet
- Monthly public reports
