# CeloHT Whitepaper

## Introduction
CeloHT is a triple-pillar ecosystem leveraging blockchain to transform Haiti
through education, financial access, and reforestation.

## The Problem
Limited financial inclusion, low digital literacy, and severe environmental degradation.

## The Solution
A blockchain-powered model grounded in:
- Financial education
- Accessible cUSD infrastructure
- Transparent ecological impact

## Pillar 1 — Education
Training programs, literacy, certifications, mobile-friendly courses.

## Pillar 2 — Agents
Nationwide cUSD access, secure liquidity management, merchant onboarding.

## Pillar 3 — Reforestation
Tree planting programs funded on-chain with proof-of-impact verification.

## Sustainability
The system reinvests into community impact, keeping operations transparent and verifiable.
